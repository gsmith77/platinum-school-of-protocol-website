About Us

Carol Smith, President and Founder of the Platinum School of Protocol received her certification from The American School of Protocol®, a renowned etiquette certification training program. She has been certified through both Children, Adult and Corporate etiquette.

Carol’s professional career began while working at various corporate companies in oil, engineering and medical. In addition, she became a trained model. Opportunities were presented to volunteer through many non-profit organizations with activities directly benefiting the youth and adults. Whether is was through the Junior League, private school Parents Association, Neches River Festival, Symphony League, Mary Howell Garden Club, Julie Roger’s Gift of Life, The Woodlands Chamber of Commerce or Texas Children’s Hospital she has held committee positions, chairmanship’s, served on the Board of Directors and also held the position of President.

As a multi-business owner, she has been an image consultant for a New York based luxury clothing line. Currently, Carol operates a full-service wedding and event business, Soirees by Carol formerly named Weddings by Design. Along with her family, they own and operate four successful franchise stores, The UPS Store in the Houston area.

Carol speaks to various organizations such as: Jack & Jill Inc., Symphony League of Beaumont Debutantes and Escorts, Girl Talk-Henderson Middle School, The Veritas School, Behind the Gates of Carlton Woods in the Woodlands, Cavalcade participants and church groups. University groups have included the Ambassadors program, athletic students and accounting department. She appeared as a regular television guest spot on KFDM, Channel 6, Etiquette Expert with Carol Smith, discussing etiquette related topics. She has authored articles for various publications.

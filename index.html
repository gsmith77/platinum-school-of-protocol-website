About Us

Carol Smith, President and Founder of the Platinum School of Protocol received her certification from The American School of Protocol®, a renowned etiquette certification training program. She has been certified through both Children, Adult and Corporate etiquette.

Carol’s professional career began while working at various corporate companies in oil, engineering and medical. In addition, she became a trained model through the Mayo-Hill Agency of Houston. Opportunities were presented to volunteer through many non-profit organizations with activities directly benefiting the youth and adults. Whether is was through the Junior League of Beaumont, All Saints Parents Association, Neches River Festival, Symphony League of Beaumont, Mary Howell Garden Club, Julie Roger’s Gift of Life, The Woodlands Chamber of Commerce or Texas Children’s Hospital she has held committee positions, chairmanship’s, served on the Board of Directors and also held the position of President.

As a multi-business owner, she has had her own Sales Agency for Worth New York as an image wardrobe consultant. In 2005, Carol founded a full-service wedding and event business, Soirees by Carol formerly named Weddings by Design. Along with her family, they own and operate four successful franchise stores, The UPS Store in the Houston area.

Platinum School of Protocol services vary from children to adult etiquette. Classes begin at ages 3 to adult. Various functions include, couple’s etiquette dinners, Girl Night Out dinner, one-on-one instruction, student programs and family etiquette. A business seminar where a professional can learn dining, effective business networking skills, business correspondence, proper attire for both men and women, how to conduct oneself in all situations both social and business.

Carol speaks to various organizations such as: Jack & Jill Inc., Symphony League of Beaumont Debutantes and Escorts, Girl Talk-Henderson Middle School, Veritas School, Behind the Gates of Carlton Woods in the Woodlands, Cavalcade participants, church groups and Lamar University. She appeared as a regular television guest spot on KFDM, Channel 6, Etiquette Expert with Carol Smith, discussing etiquette related topics. She has authored articles for various publications.
